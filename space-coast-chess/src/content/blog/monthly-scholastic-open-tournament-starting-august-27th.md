We are excited to announce that starting at the end of September, the Space Coast Chess Foundation will be hosting a monthly tournament including a scholastic and open (non-scholastic) tournament.

The scholastic tournament will feature sections for different age ranges, and the open will feature a U1600 and open section. The first tournament will take place at the Space Coast Health Foundation in Rockledge.

Entry fees are $25 for students and adults. All sections will feature prizes based on the number of entries!
